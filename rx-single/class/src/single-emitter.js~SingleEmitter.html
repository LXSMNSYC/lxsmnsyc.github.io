    SingleEmitter

    Extends:

    rx-cancellable~Cancellable → SingleEmitter

    Abstraction over a SingleObserver that allows associating a resource with it.

    Calling onSuccess(Object) multiple times has no effect. Calling onError(Error) multiple times or after onSuccess has no effect.
